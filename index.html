/ A Word You'll See A Lot: Artifact
What is an Artifact?

Inside Respark, "Artifact" is our name for these interactive tools — the thing you're sitting inside of right now. Not a PDF. Not a workbook you print and lose in a drawer. An Artifact is something you actually move through: you click, you type, you reflect, and it builds something specific to you as you go.

Every tool in this community — the quiz, the exercises in The Library, the deeper work inside Fire Tools — is an Artifact. Same idea, different content each time.

/ Why Interactive, Not Just Reading

You could read an article about boundaries and nod along. You could fill out an Artifact about boundaries and walk away with your own words, your own examples, your own next step — written by you, for you.

Remembering who you are isn't a spectator sport. The parts of this that ask something of you are the parts that actually change something.

That's the whole bet behind these tools: insight that you build yourself sticks in a way that insight you only consume rarely does.

/ It Saves Your Place — Here's Exactly How

As you type into any field in an Artifact, it saves automatically — you'll see a small "saved" indicator appear in the corner. Close the tab, come back tomorrow, and your answers will still be sitting there.

One honest detail worth knowing: this save lives in this specific browser, on this specific device. Think of it like a notebook you keep on one particular desk — it'll be there every time you sit back down at that desk, but it won't follow you if you switch from your laptop to your phone, or if you clear your browser's history and data. Same browser, same device, and your work is exactly where you left it.

/ Why This Space Exists
The problem we solve:
misalignment

Most people are stuck in an endless cycle of adding more tactics, learning new strategies, and picking up additional skills — hoping this will finally be the thing that creates the breakthrough they're after.

Here's what we've found after years of coaching: the problem usually isn't what you think it is. The real issue is misalignment — and it's happening much deeper than you realize.

/ The Alignment Pyramid

Five levels. Most people pour all their energy into the very top — but lasting change happens from the bottom up.

HOW — top level, least important

Tactics · Strategies · Systems · Methods

This is probably what brought you here — a new framework, a better system, the "right" strategy. Tactics matter, but they're the least important part of lasting change: strategies shift constantly, and if the foundation underneath them isn't solid, no new tactic will hold.

The trap: trying to fix everything at this level — always chasing the next technique.

HANDS — second level

Skills · Abilities · Doing · Execution

Your actual capabilities — what you can do, how well you do it, your capacity to execute. Skills matter, but even the most skilled person struggles if the deeper levels aren't aligned.

HEALTH — third level

Energy · Stamina · Resilience · Output

Your physical and mental energy touches everything above it. Without sustainable energy and recovery, even great skills and tactics get inconsistent.

HEAD — fourth level

Beliefs · Perceptions · Vision · Mental Clarity

Here's where the real drivers of behavior live. Your beliefs about what's possible, how you see yourself and your circumstances, and the clarity of your vision shape how you show up — more than most people realize.

HEART — bottom level, most important

Purpose · Passion · Excitement · Feeling at Home

The foundation. Your deepest sense of purpose, what truly excites you, that feeling of being exactly where you're meant to be. When your heart is aligned, everything else flows more naturally. When it's not, even perfect tactics feel forced.

Most coaching fails when it focuses on the wrong level. People come looking for the "how" when their real challenge is heart-level alignment — they're seeking an external solution for an internal misalignment.
This community exists to help you
  • Identify where your real misalignment is happening — it's usually lower on the pyramid than you think
  • Work from the foundation up, instead of constantly fixing the surface
  • Create lasting change that feels natural and sustainable
  • Build all five levels in harmony, so they support each other instead of fighting
The result: flow instead of force

When all five levels are aligned, you don't have to force results — they flow naturally from who you are. Your tactics work because they're supported by solid skills, sustained energy, clear vision, and deep purpose.

You stop swimming upstream. You start feeling what it's like when everything clicks. That's what we're here to help you create.

— Alena & Orlando
